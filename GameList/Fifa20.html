FIFA 2020.

Game Description:

FIFA 20 is a football simulation video game published by Electronic Arts as part of the FIFA series.
It is the 27th installment in the FIFA series, and was released on 27 September 2019 for Microsoft
Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch. Real Madrid winger Eden Hazard was named the
new cover star of the Regular Edition, with Liverpool defender Virgil van Dijk on the cover of the
Champions Edition. Former Juventus and Real Madrid midfielder Zinedine Zidane was later named as the
cover star for the Ultimate Edition.


Gameplay changes to FIFA 20 focus primarily on a new feature titled VOLTA Football. The mode, which
translates to 'return' in Portuguese, focuses on street football rather than the traditional matches
associated with the FIFA series. It includes several options to play in three versus three, four
versus four and five versus five matches, as well as with professional futsal rules. The mode will
incorporate the same engine, but places emphasis on skill and independent play rather than tactical
or team play.
